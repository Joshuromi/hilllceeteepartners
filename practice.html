Tax Law

It is argued that tax is the most sustainable income for the government. When individuals, companies, and business pay their taxes, in compliance with the relevant laws, it enables the government to generate revenue needed to provide necessary infrastructures in the country.

Hence, our tax lawyers work assiduously with our Nigerian and International clients, to ensure compliance with the laws on taxation. We offer tax advisory services to our clients that prevent tax issues, penalties, and reduce tax burden. Our tax advisory services cut across the following areas:

Companies Income Tax
Personal Income Tax
Customs and Excise Duties
Petroleum Profits Tax
Value Added Tax
Capital Gains Tax
Stamp Duty
Transfer Pricing

Our tax lawyers are always up to date on recent developments at the relevant regulatory bodies on taxation in Nigeria, such as the Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS) and Lagos State Internal Revenue Service (LIRS), this helps us render timely tax services to our clients. Our services also include:

Tax litigation.
Advising startups on the tax aspect of their business or company.
Advising businesses and companies on the necessary preparations that enable them to comply with tax laws.

Real Estate Law

Securing the right land or property, free from encumbrances, requires legal expertise, hence our seasoned lawyers are always prepared to provide necessary legal services for property transactions. This includes legal advisory, purchase of land or property, and drafting various property agreements, such as development agreements, contract of sale, deed of assignment, deed of gift, and more.

We recognize the paramount importance of safeguarding our clients' interests by ensuring a good root of title to the property, therefore, we conduct thorough due diligence for every land transaction we are briefed with.

Our firm stays abreast of developments at the Ministry of Lands in Lagos, Oyo, Ogun, and Abuja. We maintain strong work relationships with the relevant bodies, to facilitate timely and effective completion of land transactions on behalf of our clients.
